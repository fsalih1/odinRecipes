Tenders

Chicken tenders, those golden nuggets of pure satisfaction, have earned their spot as a beloved comfort food cherished by both the young and the young at heart. Crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, these bite-sized delights offer an enticing combination of flavors and textures. Whether dipped in tangy barbecue sauce, creamy ranch dressing, or spicy buffalo sauce, each bite is a burst of savory delight. Versatile and crowd-pleasing, chicken tenders find their place at family gatherings, casual outings, and game-day parties, adding a touch of nostalgia and joy to any occasion. From kids' picky eaters to seasoned foodies, the simple yet delightful allure of chicken tenders never fails to put a smile on faces and create moments of pure culinary bliss.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Flour
  • Eggs

  1. Place chicken pieces in eggs
  2. Place chicken pieces in flour
  3. Put bread crumbs on chicken
